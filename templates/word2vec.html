Word2Vec

Word2vec algorithm takes a text corpus as an input and produces the word vectors as output. The algorithm first creates a vocabulary from the training text data and then learns vector representations of the words

The number of clusters : {{clusters}}

Hyperparameters setting

  1. size (int, optional) – Dimensionality of the word vectors.
  2. window (int, optional) – Maximum distance between the current and predicted word within a sentence.
  3. min_count (int, optional) – Ignores all words with total frequency lower than this.
